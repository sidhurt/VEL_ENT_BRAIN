The Problem Every Enterprise AI Team Faces

This is what your AI knows about your best people.

Nothing.

Not their role. Not their domain expertise. Not the active initiatives they're running. Not the governance policies that apply to every conversation they have. Not which team they belong to, or what that team's priorities are this quarter.

Every session. Every prompt. Ground zero.

Every person is a stranger.

Your AI treats your CTO and a first-day intern identically. Same model. Same generic response. Zero awareness of who they are, what they know, or what context should govern their interaction.

Every rule is invisible.

Every policy your organization has defined — confidentiality requirements, communication standards, compliance constraints — none of it reaches the model. Your governance framework exists in a document your AI has never read.

Every session resets.

The projects your team is running, the priorities your leadership set last quarter, the institutional knowledge that makes your organization distinct — your AI walks in empty every single time.

What Unified Brain Is

Not a plugin.
Not a memory cache.
Context infrastructure.

Unified Brain is the layer that assembles who a person is, what their enterprise knows, and what rules govern their work — structured into a precise context payload that reaches every AI interaction before the prompt does.

:MEMBER_OF

Personal Brain

Owned by the individual

A knowledge graph seeded at onboarding and continuously enriched by how the individual works. The model always knows who it's talking to.

  • Identity anchor — name, role, tenure, seniority
  • Domain expertise — skills, specializations, depth of knowledge
  • Active context — live projects, current tasks, recent priorities
  • Communication style — tone, format preferences, output expectations

Company Brain

Owned by the enterprise

The shared institutional layer. Org structure, active initiatives, communication standards, and the policies that govern every AI interaction across the entire organization.

  • Org structure — teams, hierarchies, reporting lines
  • Enterprise initiatives — active programs, organizational priorities
  • Policy nodes — compliance rules, communication guidelines, hard constraints
  • Governance override — enterprise policy always wins. No exceptions.

Mechanics

The right context,
assembled before the prompt lands.

Context assembly is not a lookup. It is a scored, governed, relationship-aware traversal that happens in milliseconds — every time.

1
Intercept

Prompt is intercepted.

Before a prompt reaches any model, a silent request is made to the Unified Brain API with the user's identity and the raw prompt content.

2
Assemble

Context is scored and assembled.

A five-dimension relevance engine scores every connected memory node — intent match, lifecycle state, usage frequency, temporal recency, and identity anchoring. Policy nodes score 10,000 and are always included.

+100 Identity
+50 Active state
2× Frequency
10,000 Policy
3
Deliver

Context Pack is delivered.

A structured JSON Context Pack returns — identity context, project context, governance policies, domain expertise, style guidance. The model receives what it needs to respond as if it has always known this person.

projectContext: [{id, name, status}]

Governance Layer

Your rules.
Hardcoded into every interaction.

Enterprise governance is not a checkbox. It is the foundation. When a user's preference conflicts with a company policy, the policy wins — every time — with a full explainability receipt proving it.

Conflict Resolution Example

User preference: Casual communication style
Enterprise policy: Professional communication only
Resolution: Enterprise policy enforced · Explainability receipt generated

Policy Inheritance

One edge connects a user to their team. Every applicable policy flows through automatically. No per-user configuration required.

Conflict Resolution

When personal preference conflicts with enterprise policy, the governance layer resolves it deterministically before the prompt is assembled.

Explainability Receipt

Every context assembly generates a transparent audit trail — which nodes were selected, why, and what policies were enforced.

Memory Evolution

Context that gets used rises. Context that goes dormant decays. The graph stays current without manual maintenance.

What It Is

Not a plugin.
Not a memory cache.
Context infrastructure.

Unified Brain is the layer that assembles who a person is, what their enterprise knows, and what rules govern their work — structured into a precise context payload that reaches every AI interaction before the prompt does.

Personal Brain
Owned by the individual.
A knowledge graph seeded at onboarding and continuously enriched by how the individual works. The model always knows who it's talking to.
  • Identity anchor — name, role, tenure, seniority
  • Domain expertise — skills, specializations, depth of knowledge
  • Active context — live projects, current tasks, recent priorities
  • Communication style — tone, format preferences, output expectations

Company Brain
Owned by the enterprise.
The shared institutional layer. Org structure, active initiatives, communication standards, and the policies that govern every AI interaction across the entire organization.
  • Org structure — teams, hierarchies, reporting lines
  • Enterprise initiatives — active programs, organizational priorities
  • Policy nodes — compliance rules, communication guidelines, hard constraints
  • Governance override — enterprise policy always wins. No exceptions.
How It Works

The right context,
assembled before
the prompt lands.

Context assembly is not a lookup. It is a scored, governed, relationship-aware traversal that happens in milliseconds — every time.

01
Intercept
Prompt is intercepted.
Before a prompt reaches any model, a silent request is made to the Unified Brain API with the user's identity and the raw prompt content. The LLM waits.
02
Traverse
Graph is traversed deterministically.
Pure Neo4j traversal. No AI involved. Identity → Projects → Domains → Policies → Validated Knowledge — assembled in dependency order. The Brain owns this step entirely.
03
Score & rank
Context is scored across five dimensions.
A relevance engine scores every connected memory node. Policy nodes score at 10,000 and are always included. Nothing bypasses governance.
+100 Identity
+50 Active state
×2 Frequency
+decay Temporal
10,000 Policy — always
04
Deliver
Context Pack is delivered to the model.
A structured JSON Context Pack returns — identity, projects, governance policies, domain expertise, style guidance. The model receives organizational understanding, not raw documents.
identityContext: {name, roles, domains}
projectContext: [{id, name, status: "Active"}]
policyContext: [{rule, enforcement, weight: 10000}]
explainability: [{step, nodes, reason}]
05
Extract & compound
Generated knowledge enters the Trust Layer.
Post-generation, key concepts and decisions are extracted and proposed as Candidate Knowledge. Nothing enters Enterprise Truth without validation. The Brain compounds organizational understanding with every interaction — it never stagnates.
Governance

Your rules.
Hardcoded into every interaction.

Enterprise governance is not a checkbox. It is the foundation. When a user's preference conflicts with a company policy, the policy wins — every time — with a full explainability receipt proving it.

Policy Inheritance
One edge. All policies.
One edge connects a user to their team. Every applicable policy flows through automatically. No per-user configuration required. New policies propagate instantly across every member of the team.
Conflict Resolution
Deterministic. No exceptions.
When personal preference conflicts with enterprise policy, the governance layer resolves it deterministically before the prompt is assembled. The LLM never arbitrates — the Brain does.
Explainability Receipt
Full audit trail on every assembly.
Every context assembly generates a transparent record — which nodes were selected, why, what policies were enforced, and which knowledge was excluded. Provenance is permanent.
Memory Evolution
The graph stays current automatically.
Context that gets used rises in weight. Context that goes dormant decays. The relevance engine maintains a live, accurate picture of organizational knowledge without manual maintenance.

Trust Layer

Every artifact carries
provenance.

No AI-generated knowledge automatically becomes enterprise truth. Every artifact is assigned a status and an authority level. Retrieval ignores anything that hasn't passed the trust gate.

Status
Authority
What it means
Proposed
Unverified
LLM-generated. Isolated from retrieval corpus. Awaiting human reinforcement or explicit validation. Cannot influence future Context Packs.
Validated
Verified
Human-confirmed accuracy. Enters the retrieval corpus. Contributes to future Context Packs. Subject to decay mechanics over time.
Validated
Authoritative
Source-anchored: SAP, GitHub, HR systems. Highest retrieval weight. Policy-equivalent scoring. The strongest form of organizational truth.
Rejected
—
Permanently excluded from retrieval. Cannot pollute organizational truth. Retained in audit trail. The hallucination propagation loop cannot form.
